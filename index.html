How To Start Your Own Twitch Stream

Follow These Steps Below

There are a lot of great videos online for this that also go more in depth I will link those at the end!

Step One: Create a Twitch Account

You are going to want to go to twitch.tv and click sign up in the top right side. After that just follow the instructions prompted and come up with a unique user name

Step Two: Make Your Channel Your Own

Give your channel up with some graphics like a banner a profile picture, it can really be whatever you want it to be. There's no limits to this as long as you stay within their terms of service.

Step Three: Download A Streaming Software

You are definitely going to need some way to capture yourself to stream so a program would help. There are a good list of these programs but the best would be OBS it's free and works really well for streaming and recording.

Step Four: Find You Niche

Find something you are going to enjoy playing/doing on twitch. You also have the option to streaming many things. This is said to be a harder space to grow in, but if you feel like its for you just do it.

Step Five: Find ways to improve your stream constantly

Always find new ways to improve your stream. This can be anything from being more of yourself or upgrading on the technical side for higher quality streams.

Step Six: Stay Motivated

Don't allow the having no viewers for a long time motivate you. Stay yourself and eventually some people will come and you will form a small community who wants to see you stream.
